About Me

Day in and day out I would feel like my mind and skills were being wasted on someone elses dreams. Then General Assembly and the WDI program changed my life. They gave me the invaluable gift of cutting edge skills in full stack web development that has invigorated my life. They have unleashed my thirst for knowledge in this infinitely growing field.
